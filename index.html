How to Thrive During Quarantine

Believe in yourself and all that you are, know that there is something inside you that is greater than any obstacle.

Christian D. Larson inEveryday Power

Welcome to my CS50W Project0!

For this project, I have taken it upon myself to try to provide multiple ideas and examples of how each and every one of us can use these uncertain circumstances as a learning opportunity, and come out of this quarantine as more well-rounded individuals.

In the recipes page, you will find some fun recipes to try at home, either by yourself or with your loved ones.

The entertainment page contains various streamable shows, movies, etc. to watch during this downtime.

The books page will show some excellent self-development books that we can read and learn from.

Finally, the exercises page will list a few activities that we can use to stay in shape and work on our internal and external wellbeing.
